About PICT

Home

Founded in 1983, Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) has been a beacon of excellence in engineering education, especially in the fields of Computer Engineering, IT, and E&TC.

PICT offers a strong academic curriculum, a thriving research culture, and state-of-the-art infrastructure. Our students consistently perform well in academics, placements, and technical competitions.

Why Choose PICT?

  • Top-tier faculty and academic excellence
  • 100% placement assistance
  • Strong industry-academia collaboration
  • Modern labs and research centers
